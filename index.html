📖 Our Story

Founded in 2009, Shree Agencies started as a small wholesale distributor in Latur, Maharashtra. Today, we are an authorized Cadbury (Mondelez) distributor and Oriental Insurance advisor serving 340+ retailers across 8 districts of Marathwada.

Our mission: reliable supply, honest pricing, and genuine service — whether you're a kirana shop owner ordering Dairy Milk or a business owner needing shop insurance.
